# Pillar 5 — Store Launch & Ops Freeze (Blueprint Spec)

**Scope:** App store compliance, release reproducibility, observability, operational safety.  
**Non-scope:** New product features.

## 5.1 App Store Compliance
- Privacy policy / Terms
- Account deletion
- Support contact
- Reviewer demo credentials
- Permissions review

## 5.2 Release Pipeline
- Signing readiness (iOS/Android)
- Versioning discipline
- Build reproducibility

## 5.3 Observability
- Crash reporting
- Error logging
- Production diagnostics/traces

## 5.4 Operational Safety
- Rollback procedure
- Tag discipline
- Hotfix protocol

## Success Criteria
- Release is reproducible, diagnosable, and rollbackable.
- Compliance requirements are met before submission.
